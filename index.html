Dumpy Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 4, 2026

No data collected

Dumpy does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. Everything you type into the app stays on your device. There are no accounts, no cloud sync, no analytics, and no third-party SDKs.

Local storage only

All entries you create are stored locally on your iPhone using Apple's SwiftData framework. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to Handlworks or anyone else.

Siri / App Intents

Dumpy supports a Siri shortcut ("Add to Dumpy") that captures voice input and writes an entry to the local store. Voice processing is handled entirely by Apple. Dumpy only receives the final transcribed text; it does not record or store audio.
